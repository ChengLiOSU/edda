Return variance.

if f(x) = sum_i( w_i * f_i(x) ), v_i = variance of f_i, and m_i = mean of f_i, then var(f) = sum_i( w_i * v_i + w_i * m_i^2 ) - (sum_i( w_i * m_i) )^2.
